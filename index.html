A weekend-long hack-retreat with JavaScript developers.

CampJS tries to create a unique blend of expert-led, structured content and self-directed, unstructured learning.

There will be two CampJS Events in 2014. The first CampJS for 2014 will be held at 3pm Friday on the 23rd to 9am Monday the 26th of May, 2014 at Lord Somers Camp, Victoria, Australia.

Comfortable accommodation is provided (tents optional!).

CampJS 2014

The following outlines what you should expect from CampJS in 2014:

Top Australian Developers

The Australian JavaScript community is brimming with talent, and much of that talent congregates at CampJS. This is an opportunity to connect with the best & friendliest developers in Australia.

Invited Guests

Last year we imported some of the world's biggest names in JavaScript and web development including TJ Holowaychuk, Angelina Fabbro, Substack, Dominic Tarr and Max Ogden.

In 2014 we plan to bring more awesome developers from around the globe, big names soon to be announced!

Hack Space

The best way to learn is by doing, thus the hall is mostly reserved for uninterrupted hacking. Get inspired by the talks & workshops or the awesome collective talent in the room. Build anything you want.

On the final evening, everyone is given the opportunity to show off what they built or learned at CampJS, with prizes for the best presentations!

Workshops

Workshops are expert-led tuition to get people's hands dirty using new tools and techniques.

To ensure a great learning environment, CampJS 2014 workshops are limited to smaller groups. Workshops will repeat over the duration of the camp so nobody misses out. Workshops will be run by at least two tutors.

We're looking for people to run workshops on emerging web technology.

Talks

Talks are limited to fast 20 minute sessions, ideally presented in pairs (!) on upcoming, cutting-edge web technology.

Talks serve as great introductions for workshops!

We're looking for speakers who can deliver tight, informative presentations.

Tickets

Tickets to CampJS will be approx. $420 per-person. This grants you:

  • 3 nights of comfortable accommodation (tents optional).
  • 3 meals per day.
  • Morning & afternoon tea.
  • Unlimited "refrigerated beverages".
  • Free access to talks and workshops.
  • Opportunity to win prizes.
  • A fun weekend hanging out with the best developers in Australia and from around the world.

Tickets will be released to the public in limited batches starting 11am EST, Friday January 24th 2014. See ticketing for the latest pricing.
