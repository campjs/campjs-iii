Previously at CampJS

In 2013, we ran 2 CampJS events, with a total attendee count of over 300. We imported the best JS developers from around the world, and attracted many of the most influential JS developers in Australia.

CampJS inspired many 100s of comments and photos across twitter, google+, flickr and blogs.

CampJS hosted hackathons and/or hands-on workshops for Mozilla, BlackBerry, Thoughtworks & MongoDB.

CampJS in 2014

  • Two more CampJS events. May and November/December.
  • Larger venue capacities to reach >350 attendees.
  • New locations, bringing CampJS to Queensland & Victoria
  • Professionally filmed talks, uploaded to vimeo/youtube.
  • More ongoing, global engagement with open-source workshops.
  • More opportunities for sponsors to get involved.

What we're looking for

  • Support for disadvantaged attendees.
  • Sponsors who want to attend the camp and teach us something new.
  • More educational hackathons & good prizes for engaged developers.
  • Passionate support for open-source and/or standards development.
  • New avenues for developers to make money without selling their soul.

We'd really like to encourage creative sponsorships. Let us know how we can help you stand out and do something a bit different!
